About CSV Export

The CSV export provides data in long format with one row per VOI (Volume of Interest). Each row includes:

  • Patient data: ID, name, gender, date of birth
  • RT Structure data: Review date, modification time, assessor name, overall rating
  • VOI data: Volume name, modification level, modification types, comments

Patient and RT Structure data will be repeated for each VOI in the structure set.
