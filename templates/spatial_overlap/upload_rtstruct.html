About RT Structure Set Files

RT Structure Set files contain contour information for regions of interest (ROIs) in medical imaging. After uploading:

  • Patient and study information will be extracted
  • All VOIs (Volumes of Interest) will be automatically parsed
  • You can then select pairs of VOIs from different structure sets for comparison
  • Spatial overlap metrics will be computed for the selected pairs
